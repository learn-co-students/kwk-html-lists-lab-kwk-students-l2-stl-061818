The Best Ice Cream Sundae

  1. First, get out a small bowl
  2. Next, scoop one scoop of mint chocolate chip ice cream and one scoop of chocolate ice cream into the bowl
  3. Then, put a handful of strawberries on top of the ice cream
  4. Next, get out the fudge from the fridge and heat it in the microwave for 30 seconds
  5. Lightly swirl the hot fudge on the ice cream
  6. Add whipped cream
  7. Finally, get a spoon and enjoy!
